Prevention Tips

It is important to note that most cases of human trafficking do not result in kidnapping but rather coercion, and that there is no certain way to avoid being trafficked.

Note: data is from National Human Trafficking Hotline

1. Stay Informed

Being aware of human trafficking issues, areas, and the tactics used by traffickers.

Tactics:

  • The Boyfriend Tactic
  • The Helping Tactic
  • Isolation
  • Abduction
  • Bait and Switch

For more information on Tactics: National Toolkit

2. Be Aware of What You Post Online

  • Avoid giving personal information about yourself like your location or travel plans.
  • Report any suspicious activity or profiles.
  • Know that if your account is public, anyone can view it, and you may want to consider using privacy settings.

3. Trust Your Instincts

  • If you feel comfortable or unsafe in a situation try to remove yourself.
